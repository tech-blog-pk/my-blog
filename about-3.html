What is CodePulse?

CodePulse is a personal tech blog focused on practical, clear, and honest tutorials. No filler content — just well-researched guides that help you actually understand what you're building and why.

I cover topics I work with every day: JavaScript, CSS, React, and the business side of being a developer. If a concept confused me when I was learning it, I write a tutorial that would've helped me back then.
